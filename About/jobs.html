TRAM is looking for Several Funded Graduate Research Assistants (PhD and Master's). Candidates should have strong quantitative and communication skills and have the desire to work collaboratively in a lively team environment. Research interests should include one of the following areas: transportation and land-use planning, travel behavior, public transit, bicycling and pedestrian research. Please consult TRAM's Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion (EDI)and Code-Of-Conduct Policy prior to submitting an application.
Contact Ahmed El-Geneidy for more information. Candidates who are interested in applying to the PhD or Master's program at McGill and work under the supervision of Prof. Ahmed El-Geneidy please consult the School of Urban Planning website for details. Also feel free to contact Ahmed El-Geneidy if you have any questions.
