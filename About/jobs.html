Interested in joining ETAPA? Graduate research is often completed in challenging environments. ETAPA is a diverse and inclusive team that seeks equitable opportunities for all students. Group meetings are held regularly with open discussions about cutting edge topics, guest speakers, and student presentations. Our goal is to create healthy, positive experiences and learning spaces for all ETAPA members, where challenges are intellectual and the work environment is enabling.
PhD student applications are considered on an ongoing basis. If you’re interested, please send me an email with “Prospective ETAPA PhD student [Your name]” as the email subject. I receive a lot of emails, so I may not be able to respond; however, if the timing is right, I may be looking for someone with your skills. We have a lot of excellent professors in our department doing interesting sustainability-related work, so I encourage you to apply and connect with professors for individual and co-supervision opportunities.
