The Group
Welcome to the ETAPA: Energy Technology and Policy Assesement Research at McGill (ETAPA) research group website. We develop novel LCA methods to understand the changing regional and temporal patterns of energy infrastructure and the subsequent environmental outcomes. Sarah M. Jordaan is the research group leader. For more information about the students and researchers currently active in the group please click here.
