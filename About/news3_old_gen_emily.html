Congratulations to Dr. Geneviève Boisjoly and Dr. Emily Grisé. Geneviève defended her PhD dissertation in late 2018 titled Towards a better implementation of accessibility indicators in land use and transport planning practice. Emily defended her PhD dissertation in early 2019 titled Fostering happiness among public transit users: Analyzing customer satisfaction surveys through non-traditional approaches.
Starting December 2018 Dr. Boisjoly joined the Civil Engineering Department at Polytechnique Montreal as an Assistant Professor. While Dr. Grisé will be starting position as an Assistant Professor University of Alberta, School of Urban and Regional Planning in summer 2019.
