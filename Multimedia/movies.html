Data & Multimedia | Movies
This page contains a selection of movies that TRAM has produced to highlight various aspects of land use and transportation planning.
24 hours of transit in Greater Toronto
  • This video shows the movement of buses, streetcars, subways, and commuter trains in the Greater Toronto area
  • It was produced by Colin Stewart, with supervision by Ahmed El-Geneidy and Ron Buliung (University of Toronto Mississauga)
  • Produced as part of a Metrolinx research grant to examine accessibility in the Greater Toronto and Hamilton Area
One day of la Société de transport de Montréal (STM)
  • This video shows the movement of buses and subways operated by la Société de transport de Montréal (STM)
  • It was produced by Colin Stewart, with supervision by Ahmed El-Geneidy
Growth of the Montréal Underground City
  • This video shows the growth of Montréal's underground city between 1962 and 2008
  • It was produced as a team effort by TRAM members
Growth of the Montréal métro
  • This video shows the growth of Montréal's métro (subway) network between 1966 and 2008
  • It was produced by Hatem Abdelhabid
The growth of the London rail system
  • These videos shows the growth of London's rail systems, along with corresponding changes in population density.
  • They show the underground rail network, the overground rail network, and the two combined.
  • They were produced in collaboration with the Nexus research group at the University of Minnesota
