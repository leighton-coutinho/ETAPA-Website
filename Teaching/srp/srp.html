Teaching | Supervised Research Projects (SRPs)
This page is designed to help Master's of Urban Planning (MUP) and Civil Engineering students on their Supervised Research Projects (SRPs) and Master's thesis. Students should consult the abstract guide when they are starting their SRPs and master's thesis. When working on the SRP MUP students can consult Ahmed El-Geneidy's SRP Guidelines, these are also helpful guides for thesis students. The following is a list of SRP's and thesis supervised by Prof. Ahmed El-Geneidy's for guidance as well. All views and opinions in the SRPs and the master's thesises here reflect those of their respective authors.
Exit survey is now up and running for all students who attended the School of Urban Planning and studied under the supervision of Ahmed El-Geneidy Click here to fill the survey, your input is very important to improve the performance of the school and the educational experience of future students. Please feel free to pass the link to other students.
