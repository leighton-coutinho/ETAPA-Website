Teaching | URBP 620: Transportation Economics
Course description
This course presents the economic and financial aspects of urban transportation policies and planning. The course introduces students to impact assessment techniques for major transportation projects and policies. In addition it discusses the political debates around transportation finance highlighting winners and losers in various financing policies.
