Delft University of Technology in collaboration with McGill University in Canada is currently conducting a comparative study to better understand the travel behavior of residents. The study focesses on people living nearby a railway station. In this study, locations in the Netherlands, Canada and the United States will be compared. This comparison between countries is unique and we hope to learn a lot from each other.
