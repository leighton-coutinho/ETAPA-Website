Research | Surveys | Calgary Livability Survey 2017
About
Transportation Research at McGill (TRAM), in collaboration with the City of Calgary is currently undertaking research to evaluate the travel habits of Calgarians and better understand the liveability of Calgary neighborhoods and communities. Your participation is greatly appreciated and gives you the chance to win great prizes including iPad (2 prizes, valued at $329 each) Or Calgary transit passes (2 prizes, valued at $101 each) .
This survey will take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete. Participation is voluntary, and you may exit the survey at any time. Completing the survey indicates consent to participate in this study. All survey responses will remain anonymous, stored on password-protected computers, and participants will not be identified in any publications or reports. The data may be kept for future related research purposes.
