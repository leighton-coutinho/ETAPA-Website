About
The research group TRAM (Transportation Research at McGill University) is currently undertaking research aimed at understanding how older Canadians experience transport in their areas and what their public-transit needs are. This research will be used to help public transport providers design service in a way that supports federal and provincial objectives to help older Canadians age comfortably, safely and happily in place.
