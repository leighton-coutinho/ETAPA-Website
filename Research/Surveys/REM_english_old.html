About
The inter-disciplinary research group TRAM (Transportation Research at McGill) is currently undertaking research aimed at understanding the impacts of major transport projects that are under-construction, such as REM, Pie-IX BRT etc. on travel behaviour and the health and well-being of Montreal residents. The results will be used to generate academic articles and policy briefs that can inform decision-makers about these impacts and how disruptions can be minimized.
