About
The inter-disciplinary research group TRAM (Transportation Research at McGill) is currently undertaking research aimed at understanding the impacts of new public transport infrastructure, such as the Pie-IX BRT line, on satisfaction with transit and well-being of Montréal residents. This research will be used to inform decision-makers on how to improve the experience of public transit users in Montreal and in Canada. Around 15 minutes of your time can help us understand your satisfaction with the Pie-IX BRT line and aid in improving public transportation decisions in Montreal and in Canada.
