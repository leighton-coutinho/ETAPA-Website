Research | Surveys | McGill Transport Survey 2015 and 2016
About
The inter-disciplinary research group, Transportation Research at McGill (TRAM) is conducting a transport survey to better understand the travel behavior of McGill University commuters and their concerns towards various aspects of their travel to McGill as well as their long distance travel habits. The targeted population of the survey included McGill students, staff, and faculty. The survey will be conducted in Fall 2015 and Winter 2016 and is based on a random sample selected for each term.
This survey is funded by NSERC.
