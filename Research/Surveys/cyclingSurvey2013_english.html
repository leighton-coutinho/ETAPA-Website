Research | Surveys | Montreal Cycling Survey 2013
About
The survey is now closed.
TRAM’s team would like to thank you for your participation. The survey aimed at understanding bicycle behavior, needs of cyclists and the different types of cyclists in Montreal to develop recommendations on how to increase cycling in Montreal.
This project is led by Gabriel Damant-Sirois and Michael Grimsrud, Master of Urban Planning candidates, and Ahmed El-Geneidy, Associate Professor, McGill School of Urban Planning.
If you have questions, you can contact us by email gabriel.damant-sirois@mail.mcgill.ca or by phone at (514) 398-4058.
